🏠 Step 3 — Home Remedies4 suggestions
⌄
💧
Hydrate immediately

Dehydration amplifies tension headaches. Drink 2–3 glasses of water over the next hour.

🧊
Cold compress on forehead

Apply a cold pack or damp cloth for 10–15 minutes. Helps constrict blood vessels and reduce pain.

💆
Temple & neck massage

Gently apply circular pressure to your temples, base of skull, and the back of your neck for 5 minutes.

🌑
Rest in a dark, quiet room

Reduce screen brightness. Avoid loud noise. Even 20 minutes of rest can significantly reduce intensity.
